Recommended for Windows 10 and 11
W, A, S, D - control mouse
Q, E - Change palitra
Space(Spacebar) - Paint
R - On auto paint(no need to press the Spacebar)
P - pipet
Del(Delete) - Double tap to clear the canvas
L - Upload a drawing
M - Save the drawing
